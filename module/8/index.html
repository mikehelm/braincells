  • Month 6: OpenClaw is effectively a business partner who knows Graham's operation deeply
  • This isn't magic ‚Äî it's the memory system writing to MEMORY.md over time

2. Multi-agent setups ‚Äî when you need a team

  • Imagine having three different AI assistants with different personalities and skills
  • "Marketing Agent" ‚Äî focused on lead research, CRM, campaigns
  • "Research Agent" ‚Äî focused on competitor monitoring, industry news
  • "Content Agent" ‚Äî focused on drafting emails, social posts, copy
  • OpenClaw supports running multiple agents simultaneously, each with their own workspace
  • For Graham: this is a "Phase 2" ambition ‚Äî get the first agent dialed in, then expand

  • Example: "Marketing Max" ‚Äî always formal, always cites sources, never sends without review

  • The SOUL.md file is the mechanism ‚Äî edit it, and the personality changes

  • It runs 24/7 on his Mac Mini (hint, hint)

  • Every Monday: competitor update, week's opportunities flagged

  • Over time: builds a custom knowledge base about his industry, clients, and markets
  • This isn't a tool. It's infrastructure.

5. Staying current ‚Äî the OpenClaw ecosystem

  • The biggest risk: not keeping up. The second biggest risk: over-automating before you trust the outputs.
